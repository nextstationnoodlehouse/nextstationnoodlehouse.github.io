Why Our Pork Noodle?

What makes our Pork Noodle truly stand out is the marriage of two perfect broths. We begin with a crystal-clear chicken soup base, gently simmered until it reveals that signature pure and sweet finish, that teases your palate with every sip. Then we deepen the experience with a rich, velvety pork broth made from premium pork collar for every ounce of savory goodness. The result is a bowl that dances between mild sweetness and hearty depth, giving you a noodle experience you won’t find anywhere else. Come taste the harmony of chicken broth and bold pork richness—our Pork Noodle is where tradition meets innovation.

Paired with springy handmade noodles and finished with fresh vegetables—simple, satisfying, sensational.
